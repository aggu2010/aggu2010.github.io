Recipe #1

Sandwich

  • Time:15 mins
  • Serves: 2

Ingredients

ItemsQuantity
Brown bread 2 slices
Mint leaves 1 handful
Salt 2 pinches
Small tomato 1
Medium potato 1
Chaat masala 1/4 teaspoon
Water 1/4 cup
Coriander leaves 1 handful
Green chillis 2
Medium cucumber 1
Small onion 1
Butter 2 tablespoons
Cheddar cheese 2 wedges

Steps

Step 1: Slice the veggies To prepare this yummy sandwich, peel and slice the onion and cucumber in the round. Wash the tomato and cut round slices of it as well.

Step 2: Make green chutney Next, wash and chop the coriander and mint leaves to prepare the green chutney for sandwich. Add them along with green chillies and salt in a mixer jar and blend to a smooth paste, and try not to add too much of water in it.

Step 3: Assemble the sandwich Trim the bread from all sides and apply butter on one bread, then apply the green chutney all over. Next, place the cucumber, tomato, onion slices over the bread slice along with potato slice. Now, sprinkle salt over the veggies along with chaat masala. Cover this slice with the other one and cut into bite-size pieces.

Step 4: Grill the sandwich and serve hot Once you are done with placing the sandwich, spread the grated cheese over it. Grill the sandwich for around 2-3 minutes and once done, serve hot.

Recipe #2

Bread Omelette

  • Time:20 mins
  • Serves: 2

Ingredients

Items Quantity
Eggs 5
Brown bread 9
Butter 3 teaspoons
Salt As required
Coriander leaves 5 teaspoons
Green chilli powder 3 teaspoons
Chopped onion 1 1/2 cup

Steps

Step 1: Beat the eggs, add chopped chilies, onions, coriander and mix well Take the bread slices and remove the center of the bread with the help of a bowl. Now take a bowl and beat eggs in it. After beating the eggs, add green chilies, onion, coriander and salt to the eggs and mix well.

Step 2: Prepare the bread omelette and serve Now take a pan and keep it on a medium flame. Spread the center chopped breads in the pan and put a scoop of the egg mixture in the center of the bread. Then cover the mixture with the center of the bread. Heat the egg and bread until the egg becomes cooked. After it gets cooked from one side, flip it and toast the other side. Serve hot and crisp with butter on the top.

Recipe #3

French Toast

  • Time:20 mins
  • Serves: 2

Ingredients

Items Quantity
Eggs 3
Salt As required
Butter 4 teaspoons
Bread slices 2
Black pepper As required

Steps

Step 1: To start with. Take a bowl and crack the eggs in it. Season it with salt and pepper. Beat the egg mixture thoroughly until yellowish and creamy.

Step 2: Now, heat butter in a non-stick pan. Take the breads and dip in the egg mixture. Place the bread slices on the pan and cook until golden brown. When done, flip gently and cook the other side as well.

Step 3: When the bread slices are cooked, serve it with tomato ketchup or any favorite sauce of yours.
